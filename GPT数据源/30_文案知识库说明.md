- `01_gold_samples_raw.md`：用户当前“喜欢”的 10 条原始文案样本
- `02_anti_patterns_raw.md`：用户当前“不喜欢”的 10 条原始文案样本
- `11_gold_samples_first_split.md`：喜欢样本第一轮分层拆分
- `12_opening_library_v1.md`：开头库 v1
- `13_judgment_library_v1.md`：判断句库 v1
- `14_transition_library_v1.md`：过渡句库 v1
- `15_closing_and_cta_library_v1.md`：收束与 CTA 库 v1
